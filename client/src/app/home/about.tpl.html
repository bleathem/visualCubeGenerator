Solvi.ca

A cube scramble generator and timer

Get started

What is it?

Solvi.ca is a tool used to time how long it takes to solve a real-life Rubik's style cube. Solves are tracked over time allowing you to improvements in your progress.

What is it not?

This is not a cube simulator, where you solve a virtual cube on screen. Such simulators do exist, I recommend Google if that is what you are looking for.

How does it work?

On the Scrambles tab, list of cube scrambles is presented. Click on a scramble to activate the timer.

Across the top of the page you will see a sequence of moves you will execute on your real-life cube to achieve the prescribed scramble. The notation of L, R, F, B, U, D corresponds to the Left, Right, Front, Back, Upward, Downward faces of the cube respectively.

Each letter maps to a clockwise turn of the corresponding face, with a 2 indicating that two turns should be executed in sequence. A ` character indicates a counter-clockwise turn.

The spacebar is mapped to the default action on many of the pages. It can select a scramble, start/stop the timer, and return to the list of scrambles.
